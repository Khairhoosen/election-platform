VoiceYourVote FAQ

1. General Information:

What is VoiceYourVote?▼

VoiceYourVote is an online platform designed to facilitate secure, real-time voting for elections and polls. It ensures that users can easily participate in democratic processes, whether for community elections, surveys, or other events.

Who can use the platform?▼

VoiceYourVote is open to all registered users aged 18 and above. To participate in an election or poll, users must first create an account and verify their email address.

2. Registration and Account Creation:

How do I register an account?▼

To register, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill out your personal details, and submit. You will then receive a confirmation email with a link to verify your account. Once verified, you're ready to vote!

Do I need to verify my email address?▼

Yes, email verification is required to ensure your account is secure and valid. After signing up, check your inbox (and spam folder) for a verification email.

Can I change my account details?▼

Yes! You can update your personal information at any time by going to your profile settings and clicking "Edit Profile."

3. Voting Process:

How do I vote?▼

Once registered and logged in, you will be able to access the polls you are eligible to vote in. Simply select your choice(s), review your selection, and click "Submit Vote."

Can I vote more than once?▼

No. The system prevents double voting by using secure authentication methods to ensure each user can only vote once per poll.

What happens after I vote?▼

After you submit your vote, it is securely recorded and displayed in real-time within the election results section. You will receive a confirmation email that your vote has been counted.
